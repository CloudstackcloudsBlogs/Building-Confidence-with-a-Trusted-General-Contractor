What You Should Expect from a General Contractor

When hiring someone to oversee your construction project, you want more than just a crew to swing hammers. You deserve a contractor who brings structure to the process, solves problems proactively, and ensures every step is carried out with professionalism. That's the level of service we strive to provide every day.

At Banning Construction Inc., our work starts with listening. From the first consultation, we take time to understand your goals, preferences, and budget. That information shapes every recommendation we make and keeps your project on the right path from day one.

Here’s what makes working with us different:

Experience that Speaks for Itself

We've built our reputation on years of hands-on experience in the construction industry. Our team has worked across a wide range of residential projects—from custom homes and accessory dwelling units (ADUs) to kitchen renovations and roofing jobs. Each project has sharpened our skills and deepened our understanding of what homeowners truly need.

We bring that background to every new job, applying lessons learned and proven techniques to avoid common pitfalls and keep timelines on track. Our experience gives clients confidence, knowing their home is in capable hands.

Expertise in Every Detail

Construction is more than just tools and materials—it's about understanding how all the parts come together. Our expertise covers everything from building codes and zoning regulations to modern construction methods and durable materials.

Whether we’re framing a structure, laying drywall, or installing a new roof, we approach each task with technical knowledge and precision. And because we handle a wide range of trades in-house, we’re able to maintain high quality across every phase of the project.

Trust Built on Transparency

Authoritativeness That Reflects in Our Work

Banning Construction Inc. isn’t just a name on a truck. Over the years, we’ve built a strong reputation within the communities we serve. Our past projects speak for themselves, and much of our business comes from referrals and repeat clients.

When local homeowners talk about who they trust to get the job done right, we’re proud to be part of that conversation. We believe in standing behind our work, and it shows in every completed project.

Safety First, Always

On any job site, safety is non-negotiable. We follow all state and local regulations to make sure our crews and your home stay protected during the entire construction process. Our team is trained in the latest safety protocols and construction practices to prevent accidents and deliver work that meets or exceeds industry standards.

How We Work

We approach each project with the same four core phases—designed to provide structure while allowing flexibility for your unique needs:

  1. Consultation and Planning
    We start by sitting down with you to understand your goals, answer questions, and discuss possible design directions.
  2. Design and Budgeting
    Our team works closely with you to develop a detailed plan and accurate cost estimate so you can make informed decisions.
  3. Construction and Management
    Our skilled builders and project managers coordinate every aspect of the job—from permits to inspections to final finishes.
  4. Completion and Follow-up
    We conduct a thorough walkthrough to make sure everything meets your expectations before we consider the job done.

Each step is grounded in collaboration and built around your timeline and goals.
